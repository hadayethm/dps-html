We are located in the high country of Northern Arizona. In the town of Lakeside-Pinetop.

Our facility is capable of hosting any miner with up to 4,000 watt power consumption.

Upon receiving your miner, we are able to provide operational services within 7-10 days.

We are fortunate to work with a local electrical coop; and are able to provide a very competitive electrical rate of $0.07 per kWh.

Clients can only access the mining facility through maintenance staff provided by the agency. This procedure is based on liability issues related to the high power electrical operation and the safety of our clients.

We are fortunate to operate with a reliable fiber optic internet provider.

The cost of services is determined by model and the wattage used to operate your miner. For a customized quote please contact us directly with your equipment specifications.
